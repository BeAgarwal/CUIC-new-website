Internship


CUIC facilitates summer internship for pre-final UG students and six months inter UG and six month internship for IV semester (Dec – May) for PG. on successful completion and satisfactory performance the internship offers may get converted into the full time offer, if the industry desire so. Based on the performance of the intern the organization is consider to convert the internship into full time offer pre-placement offer(PPO).


CUIC caters to the following sectors:

  • IT Product Based Companies
  • IT Services Based Companies
  • Core Engineering organizations
  • Banking & Financial Institutions
  • Consulting Firms
  • Public Sectors
