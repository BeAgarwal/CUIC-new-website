SEED AWARDS


CUIC Coordinated and compiled the University accolades and achievements and presented in the specific format for the nomination of award on Sustainable Institute – Industry Partnership for the year 2016. The CUIC, Anna University secured the SEED (Society for Education and Entrepreneurship Development) - SIIP (Sustainable Institute Industry Partnership) Award for the year 2016 (consecutively for the second year). The award was presented by SEED at New Delhi on 22nd July 2016. The Director-CUIC also received the “Best Coordinator SEED - SIIP Award” for 2016, for contribution towards Industry Institute Partnership
